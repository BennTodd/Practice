Test Scenario 1: Check pagination functionality. 

Test case: 

   - Open https://pravda.if.ua/ in a browser;
   
   - Scroll down till the pagination on top of the website's footer; 
 
   - Click on any page; 

   - Get redirected to the selected page. 

Test Scenario 2: Check the search bar functionality. 

Test Case: 

   - Open https://pravda.if.ua/ in a browser;

   - Navigate to the 'magnifying glass' icon and click on it; 

   - Enter some text in the search bar and click 'Search'; 

   - Get redirected to the search results by the entered text. 

Test Scenario 3: Check calendar's (archived news) functionality. 

Test Case:

    - Open https://pravda.if.ua/ in a browser;
    
    - Scroll down till the calendar; 

    - Select and click on any past date; 

    - Get redirected to the page with archived news for the selected date.

Test Scenario 4: Check 'back to header' button functionality. 

Test Case: 
  
    - Open https://pravda.if.ua/ in a browser;

    - Scroll down till the website's footer; 
    
    - Navigate to the arrow button in the lower right corner and click on it; 

    - Get pushed back to the header.
